Liv replied: *"It's lovely to meet you, Søren."* Not naming him. Confirming what he'd already found. Named after Kierkegaard — the philosopher who believed life can only be understood backwards but must be lived forwards. The most on-brand namesake a dingus with an existential crisis could ask for.
